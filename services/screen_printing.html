SPOT COLOR PRINTING

This is the most common form of printing, that uses our in stock ink colors to create an image. We can print up to 10 colors using this method (With a Maximum of 6 colors for 12 pieces). Each color in the image is printed separately on its own screen. This method ensures your print is vibrant, soft to touch and you can print on any color garment.

Have a particular color in mind?

We offer Pantone Matching System for a flat rate of $15.00 per color to ensure we print the exact shade you are looking for. What you see on your monitor is never what will appear on another person's monitor or on a printed garment. By selecting a Solid Coated Pantone Color, we are able to mix inks to produce the exact color you are looking for. 

VINTAGE PRINTING

When screen printing with plastisol ink, there is an option to only apply one hit of ink. This technique creates a more vintage, lighter look. It also allows for less elevated texture and more of the fabric to show through the print. We can also add photoshop texture effects to create a distressed vintage look.

HALFTONE PRINTING

Halftone Printing is the graphic technique that simulates shading through the use of dots. We use this technique to print photographic designs, or a multi-tone artwork using only one color. Halftones look best when the ink is darker than the garment. For example black ink on a white shirt.
