SEAM TO SEAM PRINTING

Vinyl printing allows us to print in hard to reach areas- including up to the seams of the garments. It is an efficient way to be able to print seam to seam with only a 12 unit minimum. In the fashion industry it is important to ensure your brand is innovative and creative. What better way to stand out than to offer a unique print.

3M REFLECTIVE

Let your clothing brand stand out day and night with 3M reflective vinyl. The print has a highly reflective finish that is waterproof, and works on both cotton, polyester, spandex and nylon garments.

METALLIC PRINTS

We offer different types of metallic in both screen printing and vinyl. If you’re looking for the metallic “foil shine” effect, vinyl printing is the method to choose. It is more durable than foil print with the ability to print on a variety of fabric blends.

BASEBALL JERSEYS

Expand your collection, with adding a baseball jersey to your line. Vinyl Printing allows us to not only print larger than our jumbo prints, but also be able to provide a clean print across the split of a baseball jersey
