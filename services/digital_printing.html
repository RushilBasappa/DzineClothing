DIGITAL PRINTING

Digital Printing is great for small run, full color images. The frustration for a lot of designers and clothing lines are the restrictions when it comes to screen printing. You have created an immaculate design with effects in Photoshop but have been told that it costs to much or cannot be re-created in print. This is where Digital Printing shines. It gives you the ability to add unlimited colors and effects using eco-friendly inks. This style of printing works best on White 100% ringspun cotton garments.
